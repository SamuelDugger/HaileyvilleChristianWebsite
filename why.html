Haileyville Christian Church

Our services are Sunday mornings and Wednesday nights. We offer communion each Sunday morning.

Why Haileyville Christian Church?

We help Christians grow!

With dozens of churches in driving distance, why should you attend Haileyville Christian Church?

We help people grow in their faith.

Whether you attend Sunday School or one of our worship services, you are sure to hear the Word of God discussed clearly and honestly.

We help families grow in love.

With programs for the whole family, we help families grow through the example of strong families and the guidance of God's word.

We help Christians grow in faithfulness.

We believe that every member of the body of Christ has a ministry to accomplish and the gifts to accomplish that ministry. It is our desire to help you find your place of service and the joy that comes with following the Lord more completely.
